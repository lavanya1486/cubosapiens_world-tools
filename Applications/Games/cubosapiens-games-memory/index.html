HOW TO PLAY
  • Flip one card at a time — memorise what you see!
  • Flip a second card to try to find its matching pair.
  • Match found? +100 pts (EASY), +150 pts (MID), +200 pts (HARD).
  • Wrong match? -30 pts penalty and your turn is over in DUO mode.
  • Finishing faster earns a time bonus — speed matters!
  • In SOLO mode, chase the highest score. In DUO, take turns — most pairs wins!
  • Cards are Queen, King, Jack, Ace, Joker across all 4 suits (♠♥♦♣).
  • HARD mode has 12 pairs — test your memory to the limit!
